About Us

Indonesia is the largest coconut producing country. With this existing potential, Coconity was establish to introduce Indonesian coconut derivatives products where it famously known for its great quality. Coconity under CV Anugerah Bumi Khatulistiwa engage in exporting coir pith block, coconut fiber, coconut mulch disc, copra, and virgin coconut oil where we promise to only provide best supply worldwide while still putting forward a sustain and fair environment for our local communities. Hence, we are collaborating with several stakeholders, farmers and factories across Indonesia to fulfill coconut derivatives requirement across the globe.
